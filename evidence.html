Why this site does not distribute data

The Phase-18 firm-year panel and per-firm-year scores are not distributed from this site. Reasons:

  • The panel is large (~30 MB) and contains per-CIK firm-year fields that could be misread as a public ranking.
  • Publishing per-firm distress scores risks being repurposed as a "watchlist" — explicitly outside what the corporate extension claims.
  • Anyone wanting to reproduce can re-fetch from the public SEC EDGAR endpoints using the published Phase-18 / Phase-19 scripts in the project repository.
