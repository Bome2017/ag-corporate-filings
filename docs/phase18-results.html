Phase 18 Full-Population Results

The locked Phase-18 result on the full eligible-universe panel: 3,412 admitted CIKs, 36,041 post-gate rows, 19,410 test rows. Structural distress is the strongest A/G composite screen.

Summary

Phase 18 ran the locked Phase-15a transforms and the Phase-13 geometries on the eligible SEC non-financial operating-company universe under project filters (post Phase-12 SIC + facts-size + 3-Assets gates). The signal survives at AUROC ≈ 0.71 strict / 0.68 anchored under the realistic 1 – 2% base rate.
