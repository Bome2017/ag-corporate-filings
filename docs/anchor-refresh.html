Honest reads

  • Mean percentiles for structural distress (56–62) are above the 50th-percentile chance line — directionally consistent with Phase 18.
  • 0 / 7 in the top decile at lag pre_1 / pre_2 is consistent with the Phase-18 anchored top-decile lift of 1.81× (top 10% catches ~ 18% of anchored positives).
  • Joint-collapse severity at pre_2 mean percentile 73.7 (N = 4) is suggestive but tiny.
  • Divergence / masking percentiles straddle chance — consistent with its role as a divergence diagnostic, not a Chapter 11 / Item 1.03 distress screen.

What this is: a directional out-of-time check. What this is not: a statistical out-of-time validation. With N = 22 across three lag classes, no AUROC / CI is meaningful. The Phase-18 finding is not contradicted by the refresh; it is also not strongly confirmed.
