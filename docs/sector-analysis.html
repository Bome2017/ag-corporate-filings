Sector-Stratified Analysis

Per-SIC-division metrics for structural distress. The pooled signal is broadly distributed across reliable cells; Manufacturing-3 (durables / electronics) is the weak cell.

Summary

Stratifying the Phase-18 test fold by SIC division (first SIC digit) shows that the pooled AUROC of 0.711 is broadly distributed across the three reliable divisions — but with meaningful cell-to-cell variation.

Reliability flags (predeclared)

  • RELIABLE — n_pos ≥ 20 strict positives in the cell.
  • NO_STRONG_CLAIM — n_pos in [10, 20).
  • DESCRIPTIVE_ONLY — n_pos < 10.

Reads

  • The three RELIABLE divisions span AUROC 0.64 – 0.74; pooled AUROC 0.711 sits in the mid-range.
  • Manufacturing-2 (food / textiles / chem / petr) and Wholesale + Retail Trade are the cells where structural distress is most clearly above-chance and consistent with the pooled signal.
  • Manufacturing-3 (durables / electronics) is the noticeably weaker reliable cell. Possible reasons: durables / electronics distress often shows distinct patterns the G-aggregate smooths, and many distress events resolve via M&A or going-private rather than Chapter 11 / Item 1.03 distress.
  • Flashy AUROCs in DESCRIPTIVE_ONLY cells (Finance / RE residual 0.92 on 6 positives; Services 0.86 on 18) reflect very small N and should not be over-interpreted.
